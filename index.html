the following controls to interact with the map:

  • Zoom in/out: Scroll with the mouse/touchpad or use the "+" and "-" buttons on the top-left corner of the map.
  • Pan the map: Double click and drag the map to move around.
  • Keyboard controls: Use the arrow keys to pan the map, "Shift +" and "Shift -" keys to zoom in/out.

To add a new place:

  • Click on the desired location on the map.
  • Fill out the name, description in the form that appears.
  • Click the "Add Place" button.

To edit or delete a place:

  • Click on the place marker on the map.
  • Click the "Edit" button to modify the place's details.
  • Click the "Delete" button to remove the place from the map.
